Sep 10, 2016

This is the content of the article-three. Life is like a lamp. So long the lamp is 'alive' it keeps everything around bright and shining. The moment it gets extinguished,there is darkness everywhere.

Similarly a person having a good life with love,compassion and a mission makes everything around look good. And with death comes the end of a lifelong festivity.

If we make ourselves understand the bitter truth that we are going to die anyway,we may be able to embrace death happily and smilingly.
